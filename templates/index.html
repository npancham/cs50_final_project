Story

After spending countless hours of debugging cumbersome code written by CS50 students, The Duck surely is looking forward to a well-deserved vacation. For that, he is about the spend some qua(ck)lity time with his family. However, when trying to leave the CS50 IDE, The Duck finds himself trapped in a connected acyclic undirected graph, in which the edges represent possible wall sites that lie on the boundaries in between the vertices. Or simply stated: The Duck is trapped in a maze... Help him escape this algorithmic atrocity and regain his freedom!

How to play

After having selected the desired difficulty of the maze, control The Duck by using the arrow keys to move in the corresponding direction. Reach the location that is marked with a red X to clear the maze. Finish the game by clearing three mazes consecutively. Aim for the fastest clear time and find yourself at the top of the leaderboard!
